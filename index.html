Super Mario World

My first game was Super Mario World that my father and I played together. We would spend hours playing this game and other games.

Thus, my love of gaming was born. My dad and I don't play as much as we used to but he is still very competitive when playing all sorts of games.

Why play today:
The game is still an excellent platforming game and a great way to experience classic Mario games with a twist due to levels having a lot of replayability to them because of hidden keys to unlock alternate levels.

Developer: Nintendo
Release Date: November 1990

Super Metroid

Super Metroid was my first dive into exploring the unknown without any help. This game came out before you could easily find the answers to where the next and best power-ups were located, aka before the internet.

I really enjoyed the "discovering the unknown" aspect of this game. Each area was its own unique place with similar but unique encounters and enemies.

Why play today:
